About this young talented artist and photomodel

Aneta was born in a small Slovakian village called Studenec, surrounded by a loving family, nature, animals and art. She is inspired by new people, places, cultures, various art forms, and loves the opportunity for adventure that travel brings. Because of this she decided to move far from home. Currently living in sunny Brighton, United Kingdom, this hunger for adventure has brought her a whole range of experiences, connections and friendships she never would have thought possible. Aneta is now launching her career as a model and is always up for a challenge.
