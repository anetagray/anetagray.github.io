Artwork

She has definitely got an eye for detail! Since her early years she has loved creating beautiful art and the seeing the joy that this brings to other people. Aneta was spotted at the age of 6 by her teacher and encouraged to attend extra art classes outside of school hours, to further develop her talent. This love of studying and creating art continued throughout her formal education over the last 16 years. During this time she has built a large portfolio. It is from these creations that you can see and feel everything – her soul, passion and emotions – in every single piece of work.
